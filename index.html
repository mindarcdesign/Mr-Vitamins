This assessment reviews key pages across desktop and mobile breakpoints, identifying design strengths and weaknesses for each layout. Pages evaluated: Home, Product Listing, Product Detail, Cart, Blog Listing, and Blog.

This review was conducted from a visual aesthetic and enhancement lens. Much of the recommendations have a subjective quality to them. The review also takes into account the brand guidelines; as a matter of fact, there are no comments recommending changes to brand colors or brand fonts. The review is also under the assumption that this is primarily an e-commerce site focused on conversion and sales, and less focused on brand voice and visual experience. So we have opted not to recommend things that would involve a high level of creative content creation and management. It was also with the understanding that this is a marketplace site and control over the images is limited, unlike a brand site.
